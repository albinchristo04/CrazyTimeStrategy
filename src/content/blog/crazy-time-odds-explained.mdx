Crazy Time odds are fixed by the wheel design. The 54-segment wheel gives each segment a calculable probability per spin. Knowing these odds improves your session planning and sets realistic expectations.

## Probability Per Segment

- **1 segment:** 44.4% per spin (24 slots)
- **2 segment:** 24.1% per spin (13 slots)
- **5 segment:** 13.0% per spin (7 slots)
- **10 segment:** 7.4% per spin (4 slots)
- **Coin Flip:** 3.7% per spin (2 slots)
- **Cash Hunt:** 3.7% per spin (2 slots)
- **Pachinko:** 3.7% per spin (2 slots)
- **Crazy Time bonus:** 1.85% per spin (1 slot)

## FAQ

**What are the odds of hitting any bonus in one spin?**
About 13% — 7 bonus segments out of 54.

**How often does the Crazy Time bonus hit?**
About 1.85% per spin — roughly once every 54 rounds on average.

**Do odds change based on the casino?**
No. The wheel design is standardised by Evolution Gaming.

**Can I use odds to predict the next spin?**
No. Each spin is independent. Odds describe long-run averages. Use the [RTP breakdown](/crazy-time-rtp) and tracker data for context.

**Does betting on more segments improve my odds of winning?**
It increases the probability of having a winning bet per round, but at increased total cost. The house edge applies to every individual bet placed.
