## What Is Crazy Time?

## How to Place a Bet

1. Open the Crazy Time table at your casino
2. The betting window opens (15–20 seconds per round)
3. Select a chip size and click the segment you want to bet

Number segments pay their face value (the 5 segment pays 5:1). Bonus segments trigger a mini-game for players who bet on them.
