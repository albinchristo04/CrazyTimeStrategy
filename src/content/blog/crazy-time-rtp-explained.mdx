## What RTP Means

RTP (Return to Player) is the percentage of all wagered money paid back to players over a large number of rounds. A 96% RTP means $96 returned for every $100 wagered — on average, over millions of spins. Your individual session will differ.

## What the House Edge Actually Costs You
